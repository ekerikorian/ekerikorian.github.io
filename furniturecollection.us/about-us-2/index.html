ABOUT US

Our Story
Furniture Collection is a neighborhood furniture store that has been in this business for about twenty five (25) years, consistently selling quality and innovative yet affordable furniture and providing only the best our customers deserve.

The quality of our products comes from keeping the tradition of the master furniture-makers that, for many years, have created interiors to suit every taste. This tradition is imbued in each product and detail, and breathes life into classic, elegant and distinctive article of furniture items.

Our collections embody the tradition of the hallmark and design of beautiful furniture, converging knowledge and precise lines in the forms of our furniture pieces to create prestigious and unforgettable scenarios. We want to offer an authentic selection of environments characterized by the poetry of unique precision. The collections are created to satisfy every taste and form requirement while consistently maintaining an eye for detail and innovation.
